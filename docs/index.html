FAQ

Reason behind creating Beer Pong AR.

For our final project, we wanted to harness AR technology and translate this into a multiplayer iOS game. With a short timeline of only fourteen days, we dove into the world of augmented reality. What better way to learn an amazing, yet dream-like technology than to make an interactive augmented reality game. We decided to create a beer pong game that allows two players to play each other simultaneously.

Where to download?

The game is available on App store, click here to go to download link.

What are the frameworks used in this project?

  • ARKit
  • ARCore
  • Cloud Anchors
  • Firebase
  • SceneKit

What do I need to play the game?

  • iPhone SE or newer iOS device
  • WiFi connection or mobile data connection
  • In a room with normal light
  • Non-shiny surface
  • Need 2 players to start the game
